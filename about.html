Jasmine DeHart, Ph.D.

I am a Computer Scientist specializing in AI/ML, privacy, and connected environments. At the Air Force Technical Applications Center, I apply advanced data analysis and machine learning while prioritizing privacy and security.

Holding a Ph.D. from the University of Oklahoma, I have devoted my research efforts to advancing machine learning and privacy in intelligent and connected environments, ranging from social media platforms to networks and smart cities. In both industry and academia, I advocate that technology should empower and improve our lives—whether that means safeguarding our information, shaping future policies and practices, or guiding the upcoming generation of innovators.

I am passionate about responsible technological innovation—striking a balance between contemporary advancements and their ethical and security consequences. As technology continues to shape our world, I take pride in contributing to the creation of a future that is secure, intelligent, and empowering.
