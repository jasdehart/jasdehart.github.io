Jasmine DeHart is a Doctoral Candidate in the School of Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma Gallogly College of Engineering. Ms. DeHart is a member of the OU Data Lab. She is advised by Dr. Christan Grant. Her research interests can be categorized into three areas: Privacy, Human Computer Interaction, and Machine Learning.

Jasmine obtained her MS in Computer Science from the University of Oklahoma in May 2020. Ms. DeHart received her BS in Computer Science from Philander Smith College in 2017. During undergrad, she was advised by Dr. Samar Swaid and Dr. Chuanlei Zhang. Ms. DeHart has also led a teams of students conducting research on her project and various related topics.
